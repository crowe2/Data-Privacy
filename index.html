My Opinion on Data Privacy

As technology continues to advance, we will start seeing more and more software and application that will be collecting data to make our lives easier and convient. However, would you like large billion dollar companies and corporations having access to all of your personal data? This is a question that many people are having to ask themselves. Convenience over Privacy, being able to look up the closest restaurant, gas station, store, movies, etc or not give access for your smart device to use your current location. Easy checkout by allowing your smart phone to save your billing, shpping, and credit card information.
